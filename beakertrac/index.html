Everything You Need

One platform. Zero compromises.

Stop juggling spreadsheets, sticky notes, and disconnected apps. Beaker is your single pane of glass, from first call to final result.

🔬
Lab Manager (LIMS)

Track samples from drop-off to results with a built-in lab information management system. Batch workbenches, storage locations, package expansion, and stage-gated workflows. No separate LIMS subscription required.

📋
Deal Pipeline

Kanban-style pipeline from Discovery to Close Won, with weighted forecasting, recurring deal tracking, and instant search & filtering. Every deal, every stage, always visible.

🔌
Prebuilt Integrations

Your payment and communication tools sync automatically, matched to the right deal or contact. Payment status, balances, and call history appear right alongside your notes. No manual entry.

📊
Reporting & Analytics

See revenue, outstanding balances, and pipeline health at a glance. Export to CSV for your accountant in one click. No data science degree required.

👥
Contacts & Companies

A full CRM for contacts, companies, and properties with role-based access for your whole team. Everyone sees what they need, nothing more, nothing less.

📱
Works on Any Device

Fully responsive on phones and tablets. Field staff can check schedules, update deals, and log samples on the go. No separate app to install. Just open a browser.
