Four-stage invoice tracking

Every invoice moves through Draft, Invoiced, Overdue, and Paid. One dashboard shows dollar totals at each stage and a receivables-aging view so nothing slips.

Freight-native invoices

Professional PDFs with separate Bill To and Ship To, load-by-load line items — hauls, detention, fuel surcharge — sent by email or printed and mailed.

Saved routes & rates

Carriers run the same lanes every week. Save a route with its rate once — one click drops it onto an invoice as a pre-priced line item. No retyping.

Account statements & payments

Pick a customer and a period — get a PDF statement with a running balance and aging summary. Record checks and card payments as they come in. Partial payments reconcile to the dollar.
