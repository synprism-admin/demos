Who We Are

Northern California's
Most Trusted Water Well Company

Diamond Well Drilling has been serving northern California for over 35 years. From new water well construction to pump and filtration installation and service, to water testing, we offer complete services to any water well owner — residential or commercial.

Our drillers specialize in air rotary drilling for the Sierra Nevada Foothills and ODEX drilling for the Tahoe Basin. Our state-certified laboratory quickly identifies any contaminants and designs a custom treatment system — then retests to confirm it worked.
