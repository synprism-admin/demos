About Us

Grooming Done Right

We're a pet grooming shop, plain and simple. We aim to get your furry friends looking and feeling their best — without the fluffy marketing nonsense. No hidden fees, no surprises.

Our pricing is upfront and based on breed, size, and coat condition. We'll always confirm the final price with you before we start. We're not the cheapest in town, and we're not trying to be — we're focused on doing the job well.

Whether it's a quick bath and brush for a small dog or a full groom with de-shedding for a large, double-coated breed — we handle it all with kindness and skill.
