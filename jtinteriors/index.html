Meet the Owner

A Lifetime of
Craft & Character

"There is no substitute for experience. I bring everything I've learned to every project I take on."

Wayne is the owner and operator of JT Interiors. He began his career in the 1980s working at his father's sheetrock company, mastering the precision and attention to detail that would define everything he does.

When he moved to the Pacific Northwest, he launched his own sheetrock company — then steadily expanded his expertise to tile, granite, hardwood flooring, cabinets, level-entry showers, glass shower doors, and eventually full kitchen, bathroom, and whole-home renovations.

Wayne is part of every project at JT Interiors. He's well respected by fellow contractors and beloved by clients for his straightforward honesty, can-do attitude, and unwavering commitment to quality.
